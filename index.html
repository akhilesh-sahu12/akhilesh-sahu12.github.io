The biggest mistake we make is that we build our homes in other people.
We build those homes and we decorate them with love and care and respect that make us feel safe at the end of the day.
We invest in other people, you give them the power to make you homeless.
When those people walk away those homes walk away with them and all of a sudden, we feel empty because of everything that we had within us.
The emptiness we feel doesn't mean we have nothing within us. It's just that we built our home in the wrong place.
